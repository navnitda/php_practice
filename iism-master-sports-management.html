About MSM from IISM, Mumbai University

The industry calls for innovative leaders to propel it and position India as a global multi-sport powerhouse. Over the last decade, India has transformed into a global multi-sport nation with the emergence of various sporting leagues. At the forefront of this industry are business professionals who guide and lead sports organizations nationwide. IISM's Master’s Degree in Sports Management aims to forge top-tier leaders with a comprehensive understanding of sports practices, strategies, and fundamentals. The program instills vital business and leadership skills, preparing individuals for roles in marketing, promotions, governance, management, facilities, events, and participation development across all levels of the sports industry.
