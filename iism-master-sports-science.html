About MSS from IISM, Mumbai University

Master’s in Sports Science is a two-year full-time Master’s degree in Sports Science in collaboration with the University of Mumbai & GICED (Garware Institute of Career Education & Development). This course will be conducted by the International Institute of Sports Management (IISM) which is a perfect combination of Classroom learning & Practical. The learners get to apply advanced theory in practical projects as you collaborate with our network of industry partners. This will also enhance your professional prospects. The students are offered opportunities to learn & experience from live projects. The institute supports students to work on consultancy projects with sporting organizations & let them pursue their passion for sports science and excel in the profession.

Key Highlights:
