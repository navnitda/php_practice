Online Bachelor of Business Administration Program - BBA

Eligibility:

  • Candidates must have completed 10+2 from a recognised national or state board institution or 10+3 diploma from a recognised national or state institute.
  • Candidates must have at least 45% aggregate in 10+2 / diploma (40% for reserved categories).

Duration:

  • Minimum 3 years (divided into six semesters) Maximum 6 years

Fee Structure:

  • INR 135,000 (all inclusive) or INR 22,500 per semester (includes an examination fee of INR 5,000)
