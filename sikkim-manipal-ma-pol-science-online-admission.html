Online Master of Arts in Political Science Program from Sikkim Maipal University - MA in Political Science

Eligibility:

  • Candidates must have completed 10+2+3-year bachelor’s degree from a recognized university/ institution, or an equivalent qualification as recognized by the Association of Indian Universities.

Duration:

  • Minimum 2 years (divided into four semesters)

Fee Structure:

  • INR 500 (non-refundable application fee)
